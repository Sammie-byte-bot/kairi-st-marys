About St. Mary's

A legacy of excellence rooted in community, character, and cutting-edge education.

Our Story

St. Mary's Academy was founded in 2007 with a singular vision: to provide the children of Gatundu North with an education that was both academically rigorous and deeply values-driven. Beginning with just three classrooms and 80 pupils, the school has grown organically into a thriving institution serving over 850 learners.

Our growth has been guided by the unwavering commitment of our founding Director, supported by a dedicated board of governors drawn from the local community, education experts, and business leaders.

Today, the Kairi Campus stands as a testament to what community investment in education can achieve — modern infrastructure, highly qualified staff, and learners who consistently outperform national benchmarks.

Our Mission

"To nurture every child's intellectual curiosity, moral character, and creative potential in a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment — preparing them to thrive in Kenya and the world."

Our Vision

To be the most respected institution of learning in Kiambu County — a school where excellence is the standard, community is the heartbeat, and every graduate is equipped for a purposeful life.

Our Leadership
